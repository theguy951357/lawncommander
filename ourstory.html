Lawn Commander Property Services

Our Story

Lawn Commander Property Services has been serving the Asheville, Candler, Canton, and Waynesville areas since 1992 when owner Stacy Lanning purchased his first commercial grade lawn mower. A rebel who wanted to do his own thing, Stacy saw property maintenance as a way to manage his own time and care for his good neighbors of Western North Carolina. Stacy thrives on the freedom of the open air and the pride he takes in his honest, hard work. What began as a way to be his own boss, has grown into a legacy he wants to pass to his son, Trent so that he too can live independently and thrive in these mountains, or as Stacy calls it, “The Greatest Place on Earth”.
